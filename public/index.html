Who can apply?

I am interested, what is next?

Onboarding Process

  1. Click to register your interest.
  2. Provide your personal information.
  3. Enter your academic details.
  4. Share your work experience.
  5. Start connecting with students as prospective students.
  6. Wait for approval from the UniLinks team.
  7. After approval, begin consulting prospective students.

Required Documents for Current Students:

For Current Students:
  • 1. A valid student ID or enrollment verification letter from their university.
  • 2. Your CV/Resume
Required Documents for Alumni:
  • 1. A copy of their graduation certificate or transcript.
  • 2 .A CV/Resume.
